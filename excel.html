BIKE SALES DASHBOARD IN EXCEL

Bike Sales Analysis Dashboard
This Excel-based dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of bike sales, offering valuable insights into customer purchasing behavior. Key features of the dashboard include:

  • Purchases by Average Income, revealing spending patterns across different income groups

  • Purchases by Age Bracket, highlighting the age groups most likely to buy bikes

  • Purchases by Customer Commute, showcasing how commute preferences influence bike purchases

  • Interactive Slicers for deeper analysis by marital status, education, region, and occupation

Designed with user-friendly visuals and interactivity, this dashboard empowers businesses to identify target demographics and make data-driven decisions to optimize their marketing strategies.
